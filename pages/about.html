About

I'm a Music Composer and Producer of film, video game, orchestral, pop and experimental music. I have a graduate degree in Music Composition and have also delved in the study of Sound Engineering, Mixing, and Mastering.

In my own acoustically treated recording studio, I ensure the best quality in the sound of my productions and recordings. A unique combination of expertise in Music Composition (including advanced harmonic language) as well as proficiency in Sound Synthesis, Mixing and Mastering is what makes me the ideal candidate for your project.
